About

Every day you'll find a prompt here.
It's up to you what you do with it!

Some helpful tips

  • Search for some royalty-free inspiration on unsplash
  • Find a colour palette at coolors.co
  • Consider giving yourself some constraints, like limiting yourself to two colours or do a blind contour drawing
  • Find a way to make it playful, like drawing the prompt once from your imagination/memory and then again from a reference photo
