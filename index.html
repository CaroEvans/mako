About

Every day you'll find a prompt here. Feel free to do whatever you want with it!

Some helpful tips

  • Search for some royalty-free inspiration on unsplash
  • Find a colour palette at coolors.co
  • Consider giving yourself some constraints, like limiting yourself to two colours or only doing blind continuous line drawings
  • Find a way to make it playful, like drawing the prompt once from your imagination/memory and then again from a reference photo
